What the API handles

  • Requests contain a venue and market id, not personal data. A request id is generated per call.
  • Decision receipts commit hashes of the request and decision — never buyer private keys and never private payment data. Payments are settled by your own agent wallet; no key material is accepted or stored.
  • Server logs are redacted of secrets and are used for operating the service.

What we do not do

  • We do not take custody of funds or place trades on your behalf.
  • We do not sell request data.
